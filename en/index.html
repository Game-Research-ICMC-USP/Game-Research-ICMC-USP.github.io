About the Group

The ICMC Games Research Group is a team of researchers and undergraduate students interested in developing solutions for games or using games. So, we apply and create different AI techniques and game mechanics in our projects. Which are mainly focused on Procedural Content Generation and Serious Games aimed at Education. Several of our projects have been published in various journals and conferences. For more details about the group, visit the page About.
